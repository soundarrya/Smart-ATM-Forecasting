# Smart ATM Cash Forecasting & Real-Time Refill System

 *End-to-End Business Analyst Project*

---

##  **Project Overview**

Banks currently rely on manual forecasting and fixed refill schedules for ATM cash management, which leads to:

* ATMs running out of cash during peak hours
* High operational cost due to unnecessary refill trips
* Customer dissatisfaction
* Inefficient routing for cash vans

This project provides an **AI-powered ATM cash forecasting system** that predicts ATM usage, triggers alerts, and optimizes refill routes.

---

##  **Objectives**

* Predict ATM cash consumption for next 24 hours
* Reduce cash-out incidents by 90%
* Automate low-cash alerts
* Calculate denomination breakdown for each refill
* Optimize route for cash vans based on priority
* Centralize monitoring using dashboard & reports

---

##  **Key Features**

* Real-time ATM cash monitoring
* Hourly & daily usage forecasting
* Festival/salary-day demand adjustment
* Warning (<50%) and Critical (<20%) alerts
* Intelligent route optimization
* Denomination calculation
* Priority scoring for refill ordering
* Dashboards, trends, and analytics
* Daily & weekly reporting
